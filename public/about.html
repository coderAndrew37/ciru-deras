Meet Ciru

Celebrating culture, confidence, and bold fashion.

Our Story

Ciru Ciru wa Deras was founded with a vision: to bring unique, stylish, and high-quality deras to bold, confident individuals. Inspired by African heritage and modern trends, Ciru combines tradition with contemporary fashion.

What started as a small boutique has now grown into a movement of self-expression and empowerment, redefining how we wear and celebrate culture.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower individuals through fashion that celebrates confidence, creativity, and cultural pride. We envision a world where self-expression knows no boundaries, where everyone feels bold, powerful, and unstoppable.
